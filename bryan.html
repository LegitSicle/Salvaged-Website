Salvaged: The Game

Our game, Salvaged, is a rogue-like that takes inspirations from the same genre such as The Binding of Isaac, and Enter the Gungeon. You must navigate through a series of rooms filled with hostiles and find a teleporter that leads to the next level. Utilize weapons to defeat the enemies and salvage any loot out of them. Every encounter runs the risk of losing health until the inevitable death.
