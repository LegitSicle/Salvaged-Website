About Salvaged

Salvaged is a fast-paced rogue-like dungeon crawler inspired by genre-defining titles like The Binding of Isaac and Enter the Gungeon. Players are thrown into a hostile, ever-changing environment where survival depends on quick thinking, precision, and adaptability. ..

Each run challenges you to navigate through a series of procedurally generated rooms, each filled with dangerous enemies, unpredictable hazards, and scarce resources. Your primary objective is to locate the hidden teleporter in every level, which serves as your only escape to the next stage—but reaching it is never easy.

Combat is intense and unforgiving. You’ll rely on a variety of weapons and tools to fight off waves of enemies, each with distinct behaviors and attack patterns. Defeating foes rewards you with salvageable loot, which can enhance your abilities, improve your chances of survival, or completely change your playstyle mid-run.

However, every encounter comes with risk. Health is limited, mistakes are costly, and death is permanent. When you fall, you lose everything—forcing you to start over, but with the knowledge and experience gained from your previous attempt.

At its core, Salvaged is about persistence and adaptation. No two runs are ever the same, and success depends on how well you can make use of what you find, react to what the game throws at you, and push forward despite the odds.
